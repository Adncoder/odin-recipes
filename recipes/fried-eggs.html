Fried Eggs

Description

Fried eggs are a delicious and simple food to cook! With practice, you will be able to cook this recipe in no time!

The end result should have you eating a delicious frizzled egg white with the side taste of crispy, crunchy cheese.

You can do multiple fried eggs at a time, or simply tackle them one by one.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Oil
  • Butter
  • Shredded Cheese (cheddar, pepper-jack, or swiss)

Steps

  1. Warm up a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat
  2. Throw in a drizzle of oil or melted butter
  3. Sprinkle a small handful of shredded cheese, in a 4-inch area. Be sure to leave openings where you can see the pan
  4. Crack an egg directly above where you sprinkled the cheese
  5. Once the egg is almost done cooking, tilt the pan and spoon any excess hot butter or oil onto the white to complete its cooking
  6. Slide the egg(s) of the pan, and serve!
